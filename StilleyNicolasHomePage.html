My history with Dachshunds

Since I was a little boy, dachshunds have always been a part of my life. My grandma had this one named Ossie. He was so cute and was the first reason I started to love weiner dogs. Eventually Ossie passed away but his memory will never be forgotten.

One day my mom decided that we were going to get some new dachshunds. We adopted 3 that my aunt was giving away. We kept two naming them Loki and Thor and my grandma kept the other one naming him Hugh.

Loki and Thor were great dogs to live with. They were always so fun to have around and loved getting attention from us and playing with their toys. My brother and I used to let them sleep with us in our beds when they were puppies and when my mom wasn't around we'd always let them onto the couch that they weren't allowed on.

Loki and Thor are getting old now and are getting grey beards. I've also moved out and am in the Army now so I don't get to see them very often which sucks. I'm really excited to get my own place one day so I can get dachshunds of my own.
